I'm Hel and I help clients create autistic/ADHD joy at work.


Thee working world is not designed for neurodivergent people. We face a multitude of challenges, many of which so normalised we don't realise that they're disabling, that they're causing us to burn out. It can feel like we're banging our neurodivergent heads against neurotypical brick walls.

Thankfully, there is another way. AuDHD people have strengths AND employee rights that can be leveraged to create a joyful experience at work. I am here to help you achieve that.

My approach

I take a novel neuroaffirming approach that combines my background as a talent strategist, my deep knowledge of psychology, and my lived experience as an AuDHDer.

I offer individualised coaching and mentoring support, I run deep dives into key AuDHD topics to enlighten, inspire and equip, and I publish free content on the latest neurodivergence at work trends.
